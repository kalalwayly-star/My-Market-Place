🛡️ Buying Safely

  • Meet in Public: Always meet the seller in a well-lit, public place like a coffee shop or police station "safe zone."
  • Inspect the Item: Check the item thoroughly before handing over any money.
  • Avoid Wire Transfers: Never send money via Western Union, MoneyGram, or gift cards. These are almost always scams.

💰 Selling Safely

  • Cash is King: For local trades, cash is the safest method. Be wary of checks or complicated electronic transfers.
  • Don't Share Personal Info: Avoid giving out your home address or private financial details in the ad description.
  • Trust Your Instincts: If a buyer seems overly aggressive or "too good to be true," stop the communication.

🚩 Red Flags to Watch For

  • Messages that are poorly written or use automated "bot-like" language.
  • Sellers who refuse to meet in person or insist on shipping items.
  • Items priced significantly lower than market value.
